Russia World Cup 2018 Sweepstake

The World Cup 2018 sweepstake game for Odoo users

Description

Enjoy a sweepstake game in Odoo

This module is a new application for Odoo which introduces a sweepstake game to have fun with your work colleagues.

Follow the Russia World Cup 2018 matches, try to guess the score of the match and win points to end up over your team colleagues in the ranking.

Bets are allowed, it depends on your work mates and you what is going to be put on the line. Enjoy and have fun!

Players

Play with your work colleagues... or people out of your job

You can play with people who has an Odoo account in your database. But the possibility of creating players without an Odoo account is also implemented.

So add as many players as people are going to play the sweepstake, it does not matter if they have Odoo account or not.

If you create a player without filling in the user field, only the game administrators will be able to introduce his/her bets in the database, since this kind of player will not be able to connect to Odoo with his/her own account. Otherwise, if you fill in the user field, the player will have an Odoo account associated, and he/she will be able to bet by himself/herself. Just check that his/her Odoo user has at least sweepstake player rights in the Odoo user settings.

A player cannot bet after the beginning of a match, and cannot see the bets made by the rest of the players until the match is set as finished. This way, he/she cannot defend his/her position in the ranking copying the bets of other players who can overtake him/her in the ranking if they punctuate with their bet.

A manager can bet whenever he wants and modify other players bets. So the manager must be a person in whom everyone trust, because he could do tricks to win the sweepstake!

Bets

Try to guess the matches score... and be the 1st in the ranking

Add a bet to try to guess the score of a match. When you guess that, you win by default 25 points which allows you to improve your position in the players ranking. If you do not guess the score but you guess which team win or if both teams reach a draw, you win by default 8 points. Otherwise, you do not win any point.

If you have player rights, you are not allowed to create or modify a bet of a match which has already started. So pay attention: do not forget to bet before the beginning of a match!

Besides, if you have player rights, you cannot see the bets made by the rest of the players until the match is set as finished. This way, you cannot defend your position in the ranking copying the bets of other players who can overtake you in the ranking if they punctuate with their bet.

Matches

All the group phase matches available...

By default, all the matches of the phase group of the Russia World Cup 2018 are added when this module is installed.

If you have the game administrator rights, you can add new matches, in fact, you should do that to add the final phase games.

Be careful with the date of the match you are creating. If the current date is later than it, standard players will not be able to bet through their Odoo accounts.

When the game is finished, the game administrator must freeze it, setting its state to finished. When this happens, the bets of the match are punctuated and the ranking is automatically updated.

Teams

... and the national teams too

Like the matches, of course all the national teams of the Russia World Cup 2018 are added when this module is installed.

If you have the game administrator rights, you can add new teams, but it would not be necessary: we have not forgotten any of the 32 national teams.

Easy to uninstall

Once the World Cup is done, this module is useless (unless you keep creating new matches and new teams). So as it has not any relation which modifies the rest of the data out of the sweepstake, it is easily removable.

Do not worry. If people like this module, we will do more sweepstake games of this type. Any comment or suggestion will be read and taken into account. Let us know if you liked it!

Usage

To use this module, you need to:

First, give yourself game administrator rights. For that, go to Settings > Users > Users, select yours, and set it as Manager in Sweepstake rights section.

Then, go to Sweepstake > Settings > Players, and create the players of the sweepstake.

  • Player with Odoo account: if the player has an Odoo account in this database, select in the form the Odoo user with whom he works. Then, like before, go to Settings > Users > Users, select his/her user, and set it as Player in Sweepstake rights section. The following time he/she logs in Odoo, he/she see the new menu Sweepstake at the topbar and will be available to make hi/her own bets and check statistics.
  • Player without Odoo account: if they have does not have an Odoo account in this database, leave the user empty in the player form. People with game manager rights, like you, will be the responsible of adding the bets of this kind of players, under the Sweepstake > Settings options.

After this initial configuration, the game starts. Before the beginning of a match (be careful with the date of the match and the timezone of your user), players must do their bets. The game managers must add the bets of the players without Odoo account. The rest of the players can make their own bets from their Odoo accounts. They can do it by different ways, the more comfortable is from the button Bet! in the match form, in Sweepstake > Sweepstake > Matches. They will not be able to modify their bets once the match has started, unlike the game managers, who can create, modify and remove bets even after the match. Players must trust game managers, do not choose a person who make tricks!

When the match is finished, the game managers must introduce the final score editting it in Sweepstake > Settings > Matches. After that, in the same form, they must click on the Finish button. When this happens, the bets are automatically punctuated and the ranking updated. If any modification is needed, the game manager can click on the To play button, to unfreeze the match data and edit it.

Enjoy the game!
